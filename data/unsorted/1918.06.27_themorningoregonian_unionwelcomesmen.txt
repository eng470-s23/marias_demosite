UNION WELCOMES WOMEN Same Pay as That of Men Displaced by War Expected. ABERDEEN, Wash., June 26. (Special.) Women who take the places of men due to war conditions will be welcomed into labor organizations which will seek for them the same pay as that given men workers, President W. M. Short told the State Federation of Labor convention here today. The remark was warmly applauded Following this statement President Short said that the labor movement in Washington will so change the complexion of the next Legislature as to assure more adequate protection for child and women labor. The first resolution passed bv the convention pledged organized labor of the state to "the cause of democracy and freedom."
