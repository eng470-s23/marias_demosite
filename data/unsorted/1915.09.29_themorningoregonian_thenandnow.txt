Then and Now Bread and cake baking used to be a tedious operation. Now there is a machine to do it, so simple that a child can operate it. Chopping meat was hard work. A meat chopper now does it with the turn of the wrist. There are washing machines, dish washers - devices to lighten every branch of women's labor. The housewife who does not avail herself of these things toils unnecessarily. The advertising in The Oregonian is a good index to these modern de- vices and the stores that sell them.
