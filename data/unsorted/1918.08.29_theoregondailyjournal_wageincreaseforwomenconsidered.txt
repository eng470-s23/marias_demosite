Wage Increase for Women Considered Olympia, Aug. 29. (I. N. S.) The war conference of the Washington state industrial welfare commission in ses- sion here today is considering demands of women's labor unions for a minimum wage scale for women of $16 to $18 a week and double time for Sundays and holidays. Several employers have appeared at the conference and spoken in favor of a new minimum wage, owing to the advanced cost of living.
