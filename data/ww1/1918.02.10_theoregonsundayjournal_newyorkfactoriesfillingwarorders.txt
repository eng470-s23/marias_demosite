New York Factories Filling War Orders Albany, N.Y., Feb. 9 Five hundred factories, employing 261,117 persons, are filling war orders In New York state.  About 176 of these plants are calling for additional labor. Skilled male labor is called for in nearly three fourths of the cases, and women labor in a little more than one tenth. Unskilled male labor is wanted in about one seventh of the unfilled places.
