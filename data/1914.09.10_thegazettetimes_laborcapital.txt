LABOR. The producing of laborers is unlimited. Every baby is a competitor with every other baby to live. A nation is its people, not its few, and if the general Government can be called on to send its troops to strike regions, it has the right to prevent conditions that breed strikes. Women labor and child labor should be protected by Federal Laws. CAPITAL. Capital is stored up labor and goes, or should go, hand in hand with labor. It should not be recklessly assailed by prejudice, but in the last analysts property can never be so valuable as human beings.
