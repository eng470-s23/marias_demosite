Weed Manager Says Co. Cannot Afford To Re-employ Women The Weed Lumber company cannot afford to reinstate women laborer, recently discharged, at the 33 1-3 cents an hour minimum wage, and can not take the women back unless the California Industrial Welfare commission reduces the minimum wage, said J. M. White, general manager of the Weed Lumber company, in a statement to tho Herald yesterday. This statement contradicts a recent San Francisco dispatch, quoting the commission, as follow. After a conference between the management and commission, the management decided to employ the women in complete accordance with the regulation of the commission." One of the provision is a 33 1-3 cents an hour minimum wage or $2.66 2-3 for eight hours.
