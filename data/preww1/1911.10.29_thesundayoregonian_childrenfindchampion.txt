CHILDREN FIND CHAMPION Woman's Temperance Union Takes Up Fight on Child Labor. MILWAUKEE. Oct. 28 The perfection of an International Temperance Union and the working out of a code of laws prohibiting child and women labor in excess and fostering educational work for both sexes are some of the things to be considered at the convention of the National Women's Christian Temperance Union, which opened a six-day session In Milwaukee, today. The annual address of the president, Lillian M. Stevens, was part of the opening programme.
