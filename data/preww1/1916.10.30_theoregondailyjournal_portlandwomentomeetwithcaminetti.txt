Portland Women to Meet With Caminetti Portland women are Invited to attend a conference with Anthony Caminetti, commissioner general of immigration, at Library hall, in the Central library, at 2 o'clock tomorrow after noon. Mr. Caminetti will discuss with them the establishment and organization of a women's and girls division of the United States employment service. Over 6,000,000 women have indorsed the plan, which is an extension of the United States employment service now conducted for men by R. P. Bonham, director for Oregon.
